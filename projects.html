Projects

My open-source projects are listed under my GitHub account:

archivedWeb.net

The archived web adds a time dimension to the live internet, making it possible to travel back in time and see how websites looked in the past.

Pages available through archivedWeb.net have been preserved by web archives around the world. The platform acts as a gateway to this distributed network of web archives, helping you discover and access historical web content quickly and easily.
