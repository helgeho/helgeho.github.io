Consulting

As a freelancer, I offer consulting and engineering services in the following areas:

  • Web Archiving
  • Web / Big Data Engineering
  • (Temporal) Data Processing
  • Software / Web Development

Over the past years, I have worked internationally as a Web Data Engineer with the Internet Archive (archive.org) and Archive-It, leading organizations in the field of web archiving. As part of a non-profit team, I have contributed to maintaining and expanding one of the largest collections of freely accessible web data in the world.

Building on this experience, I develop innovative services and help derive new value from large-scale web archive datasets. I am also happy to support libraries and institutions interested in accessing and working with web archive data as an independent consultant based in Europe.

Previously, I earned my Master’s degree in Computer Science and worked as a researcher in Germany. My research focused on efficient access methods for web archives at the L3S Research Center in Hannover, Germany, where I completed and successfully defended my PhD in 2019.

If you are interested in working with me, please feel free to get in touch.
